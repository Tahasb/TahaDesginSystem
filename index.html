Typography

Intro

Anton is my brand typeface.

Anton is the primary brand typeface for my websites. A vivid sans serif that has long been used for advertising. It projects trustworthiness, boldness and integrity.

Nunito Sans is my supporting font

I use Nunito Sans mainly for my subheaders and my content. I chose it to support Anton in more functional moments. It prioritizes the legibility and balances the personality of Anton.

Type Scale

Paragraph

The default body text is 18px. For optimum reading length, the paragraph width is capped at 630px.
